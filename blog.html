A FEW THINGS ABOUT ME:

I'm a recent grad from Wesleyan University with honors in English and Feminist, Gender, & Sexuality Studies. I used most of my time at this top-ranking liberal arts institution to cultivate my obsession with reading, research, writing, overloading my schedule, and coffee.

My writing has been about poetry and literature, trending pop culture, social justice, astrology and readings, queerness, mythology, socioeconomic issues, music and tv, and the many intersections between all these.
